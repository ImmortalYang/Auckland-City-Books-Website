Welcome to Auckland City Books

Auckland City Books was established in 1971 and our book shops are in every little town in New Zealand. We stock books on a wide variety of subjects including Art, Drama, Humour, Music, Photography, Biography, Sports, Business, Careers, Investing, Cooking and Food, Education, Fiction and Poetry, Travel, Health and Self-Help, Medicine, Nutrition, Psychology, Recovery and Addiction, Relationships, Gardening, Interior Design, Sustainable Living, Humanities. However, with the popularity of internet, the habits of book reading and buying are rapidly decreasing. We therefore change our strategy and make our books available on the World Wide Web.
